Private School Attending Rates
by Samira Okudo, Kara Holinski, Eke Wokocha, Yangyang Yang, and Daniela Carrasco



We were interested in exploring any potential correlations between income level and private vs public school enrollment.

Using the data set for analysis, we explored this correlation across state boundaries.





Projected Concentration of Birth per State in 2022




This map shows the projected concentration of births per state in 2022 with the different states projected to have births ranging from 7,000 to 500,000.





Projected Matriculation Rate per Year (2022-2036)

This map shows the projected matriculation to kindergarten for each year from 2022 to 2036.

The x-axis represents the years and the y-axis represents the projected number of students entering kindergarten for that year.
